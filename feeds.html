CVE-2025-13539

Published: 2025-11-26 23:16:13 CDT

Severity: CRITICAL

CVSS Score: 9.8

The FindAll Membership plugin for WordPress is vulnerable to Authentication Bypass in all versions up to, and including, 1.0.4.
