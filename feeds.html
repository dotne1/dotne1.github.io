CVE-2026-8382

Published: 2026-05-30 23:16:19 CDT

Severity: MEDIUM

CVSS Score: 5.3

The Advanced Custom Fields (ACF®) plugin for WordPress is vulnerable to authorization bypass in all versions up to, and including, 6.8.1.
