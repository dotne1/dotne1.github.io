CVE-2025-3811

Published: 2025-05-08 22:15:24 CDT

Severity: CRITICAL

CVSS Score: 9.8

The WPBookit plugin for WordPress is vulnerable to privilege escalation via account takeover in all versions up to, and including, 1.0.2.
